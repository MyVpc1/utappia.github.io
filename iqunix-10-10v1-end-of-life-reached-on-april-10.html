iQunix 10.10v1 end-of-life reached on April 10

iQunix 10.10v1 was released on 2011-03-15 end according to the release schedule of Ubuntu, it has come to an end-of-support.

This note is just to confirm that the support period for iQunix 10.10v1 formally has ended on April 10, 2012 and Ubuntu Security Notices no longer includes information or updated packages for iQunix 10.10v1. iQunix 10.10 was based on Ubuntu 10.10 released in October 2010 which was based on the 2.6.35 Linux kernel, and included version 2.32.0 of the GNOME desktop environment. iQunix 10.10v1 users are strongly advised to upgrade to iQunix 11.04 to continue receiving updates or later to iQunix 12.04 which will be available in May, 2012.
